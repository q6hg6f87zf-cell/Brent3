The Core Laws

1. Death is Permanent: Unless a Resurrection (6k coins) is paid or a Healer revives you, your name retires into history.


2. Everything Carries Over: Your HP, items, reputations, and curses save to the cloud. Tomorrow is a continuation, not a reset.


3. The Box Battle: Highest coins after 3 minutes wins. Winner selects consequence from the tier reached. DM rolls d20 to finalize. Loser accepts the Domino Effect.
